Banners serve as both a notification bar and a promotional tool for your website. They come in four different status types: info, warning, success, and error, each denoted by a unique color.

Simple alerts

    <car-alert status="info" content=""></car-alert>
    <car-alert status="warning" content=""></car-alert>

Alerts with icons

Import the specific icons from angular-feather/icons to your FeatherIconsModule, add the icon inside the alert component.

    {{importIcons}}
  
    <car-alert status="info" content="">

        <i-feather name="info"></i-feather>

      </car-alert>

Alerts with actions

If certain action is suggested, simply add buttons.

Import the ButtonComponent from the Caravan UI kit, then insert <car-button> inside the alert component.
